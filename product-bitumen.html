Bitumen Stone

High Quality Bitumen Supply

Bitumen is the backbone of modern infrastructure, playing a critical role in road construction, airfield runways, and waterproofing applications. We supply premium-grade bitumen sourced from ensuring optimal performance and longevity. Our bitumen is characterized by its excellent binding properties, water resistance, and durability under varying climatic conditions.

We understand that different projects require different types of bitumen. Whether you are paving a high-speed expressway, a rural road, or waterproofing a building foundation, we have the right product for you. We maintain a reliable supply chain to ensure that your projects proceed without delay.

Types of Bitumen We Supply

We offer a wide range of bitumen grades to suit various engineering requirements:

  • Penetration Grade: 60/70, 80/100 (Standard for road paving)
  • Viscosity Grade: VG-10, VG-30, VG-40 (Climate-specific performance)
  • Oxidized Bitumen: 85/25, 90/15 (Ideal for waterproofing)
  • Emulsion Bitumen: RS-1, SS-1 (For tack coats and cold mix)

Applications & Advantages

Our bitumen products provide superior adhesion and elasticity, making them indispensable for:

  • Road Construction: Durable asphalt mixes for highways and city roads.
  • Waterproofing: Protecting basements, roofs, and bridges from water ingress.
  • Airport Runways: Withstanding high impact and heavy loads.
  • Industrial Flooring: Enhancing resistance to chemicals and wear.
